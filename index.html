Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

BLACKBOX only collects the email addresses and names that you voluntarily provide when joining the waitlist or newsletter. No other personal information is collected or stored by default.

How We Use Your Information

Names and emails are used solely to send you updates about BLACKBOX (including the mobile app). We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties for marketing.

Data Security

Calculator inputs (income, expenses, and similar financial figures) stay in your browser for modeling. If you run AI analysis with your Pollinations key, request data is sent to that provider under your account and is not stored by BLACKBOX after the response. Pollinations API keys you paste are kept only in your browser’s local storage.
