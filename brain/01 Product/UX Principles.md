# UX Principles

1. **One viewport per stage** — no nested page scroll traps; dense content, not tiny cards in a sea of black.
2. **Proximity, not prison** — snap should nudge; users must always escape a section (see [[ADR - Proximity Scroll]]).
3. **Numbers fit their faces** — 4+ digits → K / M ([[Compact Currency]]); edit shows full value on focus.
4. **Frameless modern** — remove stacked borders/shadows on media and store/social buttons ([[Frameless UI]]).
5. **Donut owns the pie** — ~70% chart / ~30% key on Budget.
6. **Long horizon, clear checkpoints** — 25-year ledger; highlight years 5, 10, 20.
7. **App copy calms down** — same type family as the tool, not a billboard.
8. **Rail on the left** — spine labels open to the right of content ([[ADR - Left Journey Rail]]).
