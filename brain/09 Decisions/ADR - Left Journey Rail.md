# ADR · Left Journey Rail

## Context

Spine lived on the right; labels opened leftward into content. User asked to move the HUD/hood left.

## Decision

Pin `.stage-rail` / journey spine to **left**; tooltips open to the **right**. Film HUD stays bottom-left.

## Consequences

- Content right-edge freer for charts  
- Tooltips no longer collide with right gutters
