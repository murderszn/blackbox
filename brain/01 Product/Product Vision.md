# Product Vision

**BLACKBOX** is a lifestyle affordability model: income, spend, taxes-of-life (major purchases), and AI stress-tests — presented as a dark **Obsidian Atelier** process film, not a spreadsheet SaaS.

## One sentence

> Model a lifestyle clearly, then stress-test it with AI — full-viewport stages, gold vault craft, honest math.

## Product pillars

| Pillar | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Honest finance** | Live recalc; loans; 25-year ledger; compact money that still reads |
| **Film journey** | Four acts, left spine, proximity scroll — story without trapping the user |
| **Atelier craft** | Champagne gold, frameless modern, bento max-fit, real atmosphere assets |
| **BYO AI** | User’s Pollinations key; canned reports; no freeform chat abuse surface |

## Non-goals

- Cloning Vectrfl branding or staffing product
- Freeform AI chat (canned prompts only)
- Mandatory hard snap that feels like a presentation deck
